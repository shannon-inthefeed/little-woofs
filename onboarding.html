1

Get in touch

The first step is the easiest — just say hello. Message Andrea on WhatsApp (07810 350376), send an email to littlewoofs24@gmail.com, or use the contact form on this website.

She'll get back to you within 24 hours for a brief chat about your dog, your routine, and whether she has availability in your area.

  • No commitment required at this stage
  • Just a friendly chat to see if we're a good fit
  • WhatsApp is the quickest option
2

Free meet-and-greet (at your home)

If things sound good on paper, Andrea will come to meet you and your dog at home — completely free, no obligation whatsoever. This is one of the most important parts of the process.

  • Takes approximately 30–45 minutes
  • Andrea meets your dog in their own environment
  • Goes through a questionnaire covering routine, diet, health and behaviour
  • Discusses emergency contacts and vet details
  • Answers any questions you have
  • No pressure — both sides decide if it feels right
✏️ Adjust the duration and detail to match what you actually do at a meet-and-greet.
3

Trial walk

Before regular walks begin, Andrea arranges a trial walk so your dog can get comfortable with her and the routine. There's no rush — if your dog needs more than one settle-in session, that's absolutely fine.

  • First walk is either solo or a very small group
  • Gives your dog a chance to build trust gradually
  • Andrea assesses how your dog walks on the lead, around other dogs, and in new environments
  • You receive a detailed update afterwards
4

Paperwork & key handover

Once both parties are happy, Andrea sorts out the simple admin so everything is above board and organised. This is where her background in data and systems really shines — nothing falls through the cracks.

  • Service agreement signed by both parties
  • Emergency contact and vet details confirmed
  • Key handed over (kept securely)
  • Payment method agreed
  • Walking schedule set
✏️ Do you have downloadable forms ready? Add a PDF link here once you do: e.g. "Download the service agreement (PDF)"
5

Regular walks begin!

You're all set. Andrea will collect your dog, walk them in a small, safe group, and send you a photo and update after every single walk — so you always know your dog is happy.

  • Regular collection time agreed in advance
  • Photo and update after every walk
  • Any changes or concerns communicated immediately
  • Andrea available on WhatsApp for any queries
✏️ Confirm your update method — WhatsApp photo? Any other app or system you use?
